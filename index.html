The Tapestry

Ideas are the threads which make up the tapestry of everything.

To be curious is to pull on loose threads.

To be creative is to take these threads and re-express them.


Loose threads imply that the tapestry is on some level incomplete, that not every idea fits together perfectly.

This may be in the mind of an individual, or in the collective knowledge of humanity as a whole.

This expression can encompass a single thread being riffed upon, or multiple threads being weaved together.

How we perceive the tapestry is shaped by lenses.

How we express the tapestry is shaped by language.


Lenses represent a person's fundamental perspective of how they see the world.

They can range from all encompassing worldviews to highly focused cognitive frames of mind.
